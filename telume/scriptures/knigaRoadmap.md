# KNIGA ZAVĚTA · ROADMAP
## The Book of the Covenant · Compilation Plan

*Tihomir Monastery, Magdalen Islands, Haldorian Republic*
*Year 3100 AC (~900 CE) · Abbot Methodius, Third Abbot of Tihomir*

---

## THE VISION

A single codex in two volumes, chained to the lectern in the Tihomir scriptorium. The accumulated scriptures of four realms, three thousand years, and eight languages — bound together in Old Church Slavonic, the tongue of the witness-monks who do not vote, who only record.

The monks do not translate. They preserve. The source texts remain in their original languages. The framing is OCS. The marginal commentary is Latin — the scholarly tongue of the Haldorian Republic. English appears nowhere in the manuscript. It exists only in this roadmap.

## THE FRAMING APPARATUS

Every book within the canon requires:

| Element | Language | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Title** | OCS | The name of the book in the liturgical tongue |
| **Introduction** | OCS | 200–500 words. What this book is, where it came from, why it is in the canon. Written by the Tihomir monks. |
| **Colophon** | OCS | 100–200 words. How this copy was made, by whom, when, from what source. |
| **Marginalia** | Latin | Cross-references, monk arguments, scholarly notes. The living tradition around the fixed text. |

**Target for framing apparatus:** 8,000–12,000 words total

## THE COMPILATION ORDER

1. **Complete the missing texts** — Claudian Letters, Tihomir Chronicle, Book of the Ring
2. **Draft the OCS framing** — title pages, prefaces, colophons for all ten books
3. **Add Latin marginalia** — cross-references between texts, monk arguments, scholarly notes
4. **Assemble Volume I** — Old Covenant (Books I–VI + framing)
5. **Assemble Volume II** — New Covenant (Books VII–X + framing + blank page)
6. **Final colophon** — the voice of Abbot Methodius, closing the canon

## THE BLANK PAGE

The seventh tablet of the Nešatta Codex is represented by a single blank vellum leaf. It sits between the last text and the final colophon. The monks leave it blank. Delegates to the Pankus are invited to write on it. No one has.

In the margin beside the blank page, a Latin note in Claudius's hand:

*Tabula septima vacua est. Deus tempestatis non rediit. Foedus non completur. Ignis tenet. Et nos tenere debemus.*

The seventh tablet is blank. The storm god has not returned. The covenant is not finished. The fire holds. So must we.
