# THE NEŠATTA CODEX
## *Pankuš Pittiyaš Ḫalki*
### The Assembly Covenant of the Grain/Foundation

*Oldest manuscript in the Tihomir archive. Original carved on clay tablets, seven in number. This transcription made in the 3000th year AC (~800 CE) by the monk Bogumil, who notes: "I have copied what I cannot read. The gaps are my ignorance, not the stone's."*

*The oldest surviving inscription referencing the Codex is the **Stela Nešatta I**, a monumental stone on Anticosti Island dated to Year 0–50 AC. Its final line reads: **𔐀𔖵𔑬𔖵𔒑𔖵𔕆𔖵𔒱** — "Remember the Rabbit. Hold the word."*

---

## STELA NEŠATTA I · The First Stone

*Carved on a limestone monolith at the west end of Anticosti Island. Year 0–50 AC. The oldest surviving inscription in the Haldorian Republic. Proto-Telumean (Luwo-Phrygian) in Anatolian hieroglyphic script.*

**𔐣𔖵𔑕𔖵𔐘**

*Nešatta Stēla Prōtos* — The First Stone of Nešatta

**𔒱𔖵𔐾𔖵𔒐𔖵𔒑** — The fire was carried from below.
**𔑏𔖵𔓢𔖵𔐕𔖵𔒱** — The Rabbit spoke to the truth.
**𔐕𔖵𔐘𔖵𔒱𔖵𔒑** — The truth held the door.
**𔐼𔖵𔑥𔖵𔑥𔖵𔑥** — The Tower rose. The Tower rose. The Tower rose.
**𔐘𔖵𔑥𔖵𔓜𔖵𔒐** — The door of the Tower was broken.
**𔕆𔖵𔓇𔖵𔓜𔖵𔒑** — The one word was shattered across the world.
**𔕆𔖵𔓜𔖵𔐖𔖵𔒐** — The word was shattered by fire.
**𔑕𔖵𔐓𔖵𔑏𔖵𔑥𔖵𔑚** — Fire alone is witness. The Rabbit runs still. The Tower is a circle that does not close.
**𔐀𔖵𔑬𔖵𔒑𔖵𔕆𔖵𔒱** — Remember the Rabbit. Hold the word.

## TABLET ONE — *Ḫantezziš* (The First Thing)

*Hittite. The creation, the Tower, the prohibition of kings.*

**HITTITE TEXT:**

Nu DINGIR.MEŠ-uš kuit ḫantezziš ēšta
nu ēpir GIM-an ḫaršanalli
nu-za MU.KAM.ḪI.A ḫinkān uizzi
nu memiyanniš kī ēšta

URU.ANTI-aš-ma-aš-kán anda
URU-LIM kuin ḫalziškanzi
n-at DINGIR-LIM-ni pittiyatten
n-at lē arḫa dai-

Nu LUGAL-uš kuitki mān
LUGAL-uš lē ēšta
PANKUŠ-aš-ma-kán anda
PANKUŠ pittiyatten ŠA DINGIR.MEŠ

Namma-aš-ma-kán
ḫalkiuš ŠA KUR ḪUR.SAG
ḫalziššanzi pittiyaš
n-at-kán lē anda uizzi

ENIM-aš-ma-aš-kán URU-aš
URU-aš-ma-kán ENIM-aš
kī-ma ŪL kappuuanzi
pittiyaš-ma kī

---

**GLOSS — TABLET ONE:**

*Nu DINGIR.MEŠ-uš kuit ḫantezziš ēšta*
"Now — what was the first thing among the gods"
— Every Hittite legal document opens with *nu* — "now, and so, therefore." Not a word for present time but a logical connector. The text begins in the middle of a thought that started before writing.

*nu ēpir GIM-an ḫaršanalli*
"and they took it as one takes a skull-cup"
— *ḫaršanalli* — a ritual vessel made from the skull of a defeated enemy. The first thing the gods took was taken the way victors take trophies.

*nu-za MU.KAM.ḪI.A ḫinkān uizzi*
"and the years come toward death"
— Years don't pass in Hittite — they *come toward* you. Time arrives.

*nu memiyanniš kī ēšta*
"and the word — this — was"
— *memiyanni* — the word, the matter, the thing spoken. The Hittite word for language is also the word for legal matter.

*URU.ANTI-aš-ma-aš-kán anda*
"But as for the city that stood against —"
— The Tower. Named not by its builders' name but by its relationship to the gods.

*URU-LIM kuin ḫalziškanzi*
"which they kept calling the great city"
— Iterative verb form. The name was an act of will repeated until it became true.

*n-at DINGIR-LIM-ni pittiyatten*
"entrust it — do not — to the god"
— The syntax breaks here. The tablet is damaged or the scribe hesitated.

*Nu LUGAL-uš kuitki mān*
"Now as for the king — if there were such a thing —"
— The king is introduced as a conditional. *If* there were a king.

*LUGAL-uš lē ēšta*
"a king shall not be"
— The central prohibition of the Pankus. Not "there is no king" but "a king shall not be."

*PANKUŠ-aš-ma-kán anda*
"but the assembly — within —"
— The Pankus as container. You are inside the assembly the way you are inside a building.

*PANKUŠ pittiyatten ŠA DINGIR.MEŠ*
"entrust the assembly to the gods"
— The sentence that was broken now completes itself. The assembly, not the city, is the sacred object.

*ḫalkiuš ŠA KUR ḪUR.SAG*
"the grain of the mountain land"
— *Ḫalki* — grain and foundation are the same word. What feeds you and what holds you up.

*ḫalziššanzi pittiyaš*
"they shall keep calling the covenant"
— The same iterative verb used for the Tower. Now applied to the covenant itself.

*n-at-kán lē anda uizzi*
"and it shall not come within"
— What shall not enter? The text does not say. The ambiguity is the law.

*ENIM-aš-ma-aš-kán URU-aš*
"as for the word — as for the city"
*URU-aš-ma-kán ENIM-aš*
"as for the city — as for the word"
— Mirror construction. Word and city reflect each other.

*kī-ma ŪL kappuuanzi*
"and these are not counted"
— The assembly, the covenant, the foundation — they are not countable.

*pittiyaš-ma kī*
"this is the covenant"

## TABLET TWO — *Luwian Intrusion*

*Luwian. The sun god's triple position, the chiasm, the storm god's departure.*

*The monk Bogumil notes: "Here the script changes. I have done my best. The second tablet is not Hittite. It is something older or something adjacent. I have transliterated as faithfully as I am able."*

**LUWIAN TEXT:**

Tiwad-ati ḫantawatin
piran ḫuiššuwanza
Tiwad-ati uratin
arḫa ḫuiššuwanza

Ura pittiyanza
pittiyanza ura
kī ḫandanza
ḫandanza kī

Tarḫunt-ati wāti
wāti Tarḫunt-ati
mān ura pittiyai
mān pittiyai ura

Tiwad-anza ḫantaš
ḫantaš Tiwad-anza
ura-anza pittiyaš
pittiyaš ura-anza

---

**GLOSS — TABLET TWO:**

*Tiwad-ati ḫantawatin*
"The sun-god — in front of the lord"
— *Tiwad* — the Luwian sun deity, male. The shift from Hittite to Luwian is also a shift in solar theology.

*piran ḫuiššuwanza*
"living in front, alive before"
— The sun god precedes authority. The sun as precedent.

*Tiwad-ati uratin*
"The sun-god — behind the great one"
— The same sun god now behind the great one. Two consecutive lines place the sun before and behind power simultaneously.

*arḫa ḫuiššuwanza*
"living away, alive apart"
— The third position: separated from authority entirely. Before, behind, and apart.

*Ura pittiyanza*
"The great covenant"
— *Ura* — great. *Pittiyanza* — the covenanted thing. In Luwian the covenant is a noun, not a verb. It exists.

*pittiyanza ura*
"covenanted — great"
— Reversed. The chiasm begins. The great covenant and the covenanted greatness are not the same thing but they rhyme.

*kī ḫandanza*
"this — arranged, set in order"
— What was arranged. The arranger is absent from the text. Passive construction.

*ḫandanza kī*
"arranged — this"
— Reversed again. The order and the thing ordered face each other.

*Tarḫunt-ati wāti*
"The storm god — he went"
— Tarḫunt departed. The theological crisis at the center of the Codex.

*wāti Tarḫunt-ati*
"He went — the storm god"
— The departure stated twice in mirror form, as if reversal could undo it.

*mān ura pittiyai*
"if the great one covenants"
— Suddenly conditional. Did the covenant happen?

*mān pittiyai ura*
"if the covenant is great"
— The condition reversed. Two separate doubts in mirror form.

*Tiwad-anza ḫantaš*
"The sun-god — arranged"
*ḫantaš Tiwad-anza*
"Arranged — the sun-god"
*ura-anza pittiyaš*
"great — covenanted"
*pittiyaš ura-anza*
"covenanted — great"
— The closing chiasm. Sun and covenant in perpetual mirror relation.

## TABLET THREE — *Ḫalkiš* (The Grain / The Foundation)

*Hittite. The triple covenant. The assembly entrusted to the land. The closing seal.*

**HITTITE TEXT:**

Nu-za kī memiyanniš
kī pittiyaš
kī URU.ANTI-aš
lē šarrater

URU-aš-ma-kán anda
pittiyaš-ma-kán anda
ENIM-aš-ma-kán anda
n-at-kán lē arḫa

Nu kī PANKUŠ-aš
nu kī pittiyaš
nu kī ḫalkiš
n-at ŪL kappuuanzi

GIM-an-ma-kán
MU.KAM.ḪI.A ḫinkān uizzi
n-at-kán PANKUŠ-ni anda
pittiyatten

Nu-za lē LUGAL-uš
nu-za lē LUGAL.GAL-uš
PANKUŠ-aš-ma-za
pittiyatten ŠA KUR

Namma-ma-kán
ḫalkiuš pittiyaš
ḫalziššanzi memiyanniš
n-at lē šarrater

---

**GLOSS — TABLET THREE:**

*Nu-za kī memiyanniš*
"Now — this word/matter"
— The *-za* particle — reflexive. The text turning back on itself.

*kī pittiyaš*
"this covenant"
— *Kī* — this, proximal. Not "that covenant" (distant) but "this covenant" (present). The text insists on its own immediacy.

*kī URU.ANTI-aš*
"this city-that-stood-against"
— The Tower named again, but now with *kī* — proximate. The Collapse is not past. It is present.

*lē šarrater*
"shall not be broken"
— The most serious prohibition in Hittite law. Applied to the covenant itself.

*URU-aš-ma-kán anda*
"as for the city — within"
*pittiyaš-ma-kán anda*
"as for the covenant — within"
*ENIM-aš-ma-kán anda*
"as for the word — within"
— Three parallel constructions. City, covenant, word — all containers.

*n-at-kán lē arḫa*
"and they shall not be taken away"
— The central legal claim: the three are bound together permanently.

*Nu kī PANKUŠ-aš*
"Now — this assembly"
*nu kī pittiyaš*
"Now — this covenant"
*nu kī ḫalkiš*
"Now — this grain / this foundation"
— Triple *nu kī*. The assembly, the covenant, the foundation.

*n-at ŪL kappuuanzi*
"and these are not counted"
— They exist beyond measurement.

*GIM-an-ma-kán*
"But just as —"
*MU.KAM.ḪI.A ḫinkān uizzi*
"the years come toward death"
*n-at-kán PANKUŠ-ni anda*
"so within the assembly"
— The years come toward death. So within the assembly — the tablet breaks here.

*Nu-za lē LUGAL-uš*
"Now — there shall not be a king"
*nu-za lē LUGAL.GAL-uš*
"Now — there shall not be a great king"
— Prohibition twice, escalating.

*PANKUŠ-aš-ma-za*
"but the assembly — for itself"
— Self-governing, self-referential.

*pittiyatten ŠA KUR*
"you shall entrust to the land"
— The assembly entrusted to the land itself. The water as authority.

*Namma-ma-kán*
"Furthermore —"
*ḫalkiuš pittiyaš*
"the covenant of the grain/foundation"
— The document names itself.

*ḫalziššanzi memiyanniš*
"they shall keep calling the word"
— The iterative verb. Keep speaking it. The reading is the covenanting.

*n-at lē šarrater*
"and it shall not be broken"
— The closing seal. The same prohibition as the opening.

## COLOPHON

*In the hand of Bogumil, Tihomir monastery, Year 3000 AC (~800 CE):*

"Seven tablets. I have copied three. The fourth is missing — removed before my time, the abbot says, by a delegation from Tsarnigrad who came in the night and left before morning. The fifth is in a script I do not recognize — not Hittite, not Luwian, not any hand I have been trained to read. The sixth is intact but sealed with clay impressed with a glyph I will not reproduce here. The seventh tablet is blank. The clay was fired. The surface was prepared. Nothing was written on it. I have copied the blank tablet as a blank page. The blank page is here. You are reading the blank tablet now. I do not know what this means. The abbot says it means the covenant is not finished. The storm god has not returned to write the final tablet. We are waiting. The Pankus has been waiting since before our order existed. The fire holds."

*𒁹𒀭𒊏 𒁹𒋾𒀭 𒁹𒀭𒍪*
*PITTIYA · PITTISSA · PITTAZU*
*The covenant was. The covenant broke. The covenant holds.*

---

*Senator Claudius, Year 3400 AC (~1200 CE), adds in the margin of the Tihomir copy:*

"I have read what I cannot save. The seventh tablet is still blank. The storm god has not returned. The Pankus meets again in seven years — if the ice allows it. I am an old man. I will not see another assembly. I leave this note as my vote: hold the covenant. The fire holds. So must we."
