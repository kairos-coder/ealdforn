The Legislative Pipeline
I
The Oracle Speaks
Augur Maximus · Observatory
The Ephemeris Publica is published at dawn. The sky-state is recorded. This phase is automatic — the Oracle does not propose, only informs. The Comitia's Century of the Watch validates the Chthonic threshold.
II
The Comitia Proposes
300 Citizens · Forum of Caepton
A measure is debated and voted upon by raised hands. If it passes by simple majority (151 of 300), it becomes a Plebiscitum. Hecate's influence is most potent here, testing the minds of the citizenry.
III
The Senate Ratifies or Rejects
12 Senators · Senate Chamber
The Senate receives the Plebiscitum. If it passes (7 of 12), it becomes law. If the Senate ties 6-6, the Aquarian Senator casts the tiebreaker. At any point before ratification, the Tribune may issue the Sacred Veto.

Eligibility & Sortition

The Sky-Vigil. Every citizen who wishes to stand for the Comitia must complete one year of nightly sky observation. The completed vigil is submitted to the Abbey for the Censor's review.

Refusal. A citizen whose name is drawn in the Solstice Sortition may refuse service. The refusal is recorded in the Charon Archive as "one who turned away from the Republic's call." This is not a crime. It is not a punishment. It is simply remembered.
