⌂ About the Oracle

The Oracle of Glimthaven was built before the Republic. Before the Constitution. Before the first Praetor took the first throne. The astronomers were watching the sky while the founders were still arguing in the Hall of Seven Thrones.

The Oracle does not govern. It informs. The Ephemeris Publica is published daily and distributed to the Court, the Senate, the Comitia, and the shrines. Citizens may purchase copies at the Forum for one copper token.

The Oracle is led by the Augur Maximus, who sits on both the Oracle and the Libraen Court — the bridge between prediction and action. The Calculatorium computes. The Sign-Watchers watch. The Augur interprets. The Republic endures.
