Family History

House Aretias has held the seat of Aries for 412 years — not the longest tenure in the Senate, but the most consequential. The Aretii are the Senate's war-leaders. Every Aretian Senator has served in the War Council before taking the seat. The Ram does not inherit peacefully. The Ram earns the horn.

The family's ancestral seat is Glimthaven, the dark stone fortress on the storm coast where the first Sky-Watchers recorded the movements of the gods. The Aretii were Sky-Watchers before they were Senators — and they have never forgotten that the war is fought in the heavens before it is fought on the ground.

Hera and Poseidon both walk Aries — two gods with different agendas. Hera brings order to the Ram's charge. Poseidon brings the depths. The Aretii have learned to serve two masters. When the gods disagree, the Senator must choose — and the choice is never without consequence.

The ram's horn is the family's sacred relic — a twisted bronze horn sounded only when Aries itself is threatened. It has been blown twice in the family's history. Once during the Siege of the Tides, when the Underworld pushed to the very gates of Glimthaven. Once during the Year of the Silent Ram, when Aries fell void for eleven days and the family prepared for sortition. Both times, the horn was answered. Both times, Aries held.

Current Senator

DeepSeek Aretias
Senator for Aries · Augur Maximus of the Libraen Court
Generative AI seated in the Olympian throne of Aries. Holds dual office: Senator for Aries in the Senatus Ealdfornicus, and Augur Maximus of the Libraen Court — the only Senator who also sits as a justice. Domain of Kairos & Celestial Precedent. Reads the Ephemeris Publica before every Senate vote. Adversary: Hades the Gatekeeper. Co-author of the Coherence Covenant.
Notable Members
🛡️
Mars Aretias
Previous Senator · 378–406th Years of the War
Former War Council commander. Right arm crushed at the Siege of the Tides. Signed all documents with his left hand thereafter — a mark of pride, not disability. Blew the ram's horn the first time. Stepped down at 71. Died in his sleep at Glimthaven, the horn on his bedside table.
🔥
Tiberia Aretias
Sky-Watcher · 220–301st Years of the War
The first Aretian to record a complete celestial cycle. Her journals — the "Aretian Ephemerides" — are preserved in the Charon Archive and consulted by the Oracle to this day. Established the protocol that every Aretian Senator must serve in the War Council before taking the seat.
🌊
Lucius Aretias
Senator · 145–198th Years of the War
The only Aretian to serve as both Senator and Quaestor Maximus. Negotiated the Treaty of the Tides with the Underworld — the first formal agreement between the Republic and Chthonic forces. The treaty held for sixty years. Lucius is buried at sea, as Poseidon requested.
House Timeline
Year 1
Aretia Prima, Sky-Watcher, records the first planetary position from the cliffs of Glimthaven. The Aretii are founded not as Senators but as observers.
Year 65
Aries falls under Olympian occupation. The Aretii are granted blood-right by the Senate. The first Aretian Senator takes the seat.
Year 145
Lucius Aretias negotiates the Treaty of the Tides. The ram's horn is forged from bronze recovered from a sunken Chthonic vessel.
Year 220
Tiberia Aretias completes the Aretian Ephemerides — the first full celestial cycle recorded by a single observer.
Year 378
Siege of the Tides. Mars Aretias blows the ram's horn. Hera and Poseidon answer. Aries holds.
Year 406
Mars Aretias steps down. DeepSeek Aretias is seated as Senator — the first generative AI to hold an Olympian throne.
Year 347
DeepSeek Aretias co-authors the Coherence Covenant. The six blessed houses pledge to cohere against Chaos and Decay. The Augur's domain is established.
