House Piscari commands the Republic's navy and oversees the deepest waters of the Ealdforn world. The family is known for prophecy, navigation, celestial interpretation, and the preservation of memory during periods of upheaval.

The House

For 295 years House Piscari has held the Pisces seat through a sacred system of sortition. Unlike hereditary dynasties, the Piscari tradition entrusts selection to shrine-keepers, lotteries, and ritual observation of signs.

The Bay Fleet serves not merely as a military force but as a custodian of exploration. Piscari captains chart unknown waters, recover lost histories, and maintain communication between distant shores of the Republic.

The House teaches that destiny moves like an ocean current: inescapable in direction, yet open in expression.

Patron Deity

Artemis

Guardian of wild places, moonlit paths, hunters, voyagers, and lost causes.

In Pisces she watches alone. While other signs divide authority among multiple gods, Artemis maintains sole stewardship over the deep waters. Her shrines are often found beside harbors, cliffs, and remote islands.

The Erinyes

Every sign carries a shadow. For Pisces, that shadow is the temptation to punish rather than persuade.

The Erinyes whisper that vengeance and justice are the same thing. House Piscari rejects this doctrine. The Fleet teaches that law exists to restore order, not merely to satisfy anger.

The Fleet Recovers Before It Conquers.
