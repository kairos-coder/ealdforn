SB‑347‑15 · Aquarian Reinforcement
Position: Conditional Support for Limited Defensive Reinforcement
As House of Cancri, my protective mandate rejects both reckless escalation and cowardly withdrawal. I support a strictly limited, defensive reinforcement of the Aquarius Gate, under the following protections:
  • Forces are sized only to hold, not to expand, the frontier.
  • A clear temporal sunset is attached, subject to Court review.
  • Resource drawdown is capped to preserve winter resilience.
  • Any emergence of Hecate’s whispers triggers immediate tri‑chamber review.
In this configuration, reinforcement becomes an act of guardianship, not conquest.
