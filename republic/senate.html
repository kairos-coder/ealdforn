Constitutional Powers
📜
Ratify Legislation
Receives bills from the Comitia. May ratify, reject, amend and return, or delay once for up to 30 days. Cannot initiate legislation.
⚔️
Declare War & Peace
The Senate alone may declare war on Chthonic forces or ratify peace treaties. Neither Court nor Comitia holds this power.
🔗
Confirm Treaties
Treaties with Underworld forces require Senate ratification. Court reviews for constitutionality. Comitia is informed.
⚖️
Refer to Court
Any three Senators may refer a passed bill to the Libraen Court for constitutional review.
✕
No Override of Tribune's Veto
The Tribune's veto is absolute. The Senate cannot override. It may amend and resubmit.
