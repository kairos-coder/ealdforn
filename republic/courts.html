Judicial Review · Procedures

⬡ Triggers — How Review Begins ⬡

🏛️
Senatorial Referral
Any three Senators may refer a passed bill. Must cite constitutional principle violated.
🗳️
Comitial Petition
Fifty citizens or the Tribune may petition for review. Tribune limited to once per session.
⚖️
Judicial Notice
Any justice may raise notice. Requires second justice to concur before review proceeds.
⬡
Kairotic Emergency
Augur Maximus triggers review for critical celestial configurations. Carries weight of necessity.

⬡ Standards of Review ⬡

✕
Constitutional Scrutiny
5 votes required. Permanent nullification. Highest standard.
↩
Threshold Review
4 votes required. Bill returned with mandated amendments.
⏳
Kairotic Scrutiny
3 votes required. Delayed or amended for celestial timing. Liminal Bench doctrine.
📜
Advisory Opinion
3 votes required. Non-binding. Non-compliance archived in Charon.
